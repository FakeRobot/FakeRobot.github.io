Statistics

Below are some important facts and statistics about boring, abusive, and crazy teachers and thier effects:

Statiscics
Boring - 95% of all students say that they hajve had a teacher so boring that they didn't want to come to class
- 50% of all students admit to have skipped class becuase they didn't want to go to a class with a boring teacher
- 75% of students admit to having fallen asleep during a boring lecture
Abusive - 50% of all students claim to have had a verbally or physically abusive teacher
- 60% of all students said that they have not answered questions in class because they were scared to get mocked by thier teacher
Crazy - 25% of students have claimed to have a mentally unsound teacher
Facts
Boring - Students learn less when thier teacher speaks in a monotone.
- Students are more likely to skip class if they think they have a boring teacher
Abusive - Having an abusive teacher causes students to not ask questions, and therefore not learn all that they need to know
Crazy - Crazy teachers are more likely to not teach the required material
- Students with crazy teachers have little respect for the teacher and if they don't respect thier teacher they are more likley to skip class or not pay attention
