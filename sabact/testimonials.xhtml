This page is devoted to the testimonials of angry students and parents. Names have been changed and school names omitted to protect the teachers and the students.

Amanda, 19, Canada

I had a professor last semester that would ridicule anyone that would fall asleep or not pay attention. This was often becuase I had the class at 8:30 am. I eventually stopped going because I didn't want to be the object of his verbal abuse.

Brett, 15, Canada

My art teacher was crazy. She had no control over the class. She would turn around and try and block us out or just leave the class halfway through. I didn't learn a thing, but I did get a good mark because I drew a cat for my final project.

Wang, 22, USA

I failed my Math 252 class in my second year becuase of a boring teacher. She was monotone, only dressed in grey, and never moved out from behind her desk. Someone actually brought a pillow and blanket to the class and slept. She didn't even notice. I can count the number of time she looked up from her notes on one hand.

Todd, 18, Great Britain

My professor was a real wanker. He would give pop quizzes on unrelated subjects and count them towards attendance marks. Why would I know the answers to ten questions about free-trade coffee? I was in a Computer Science class. He did other crazy stuff too. Once he ate a piece of chalk just to get people's attention. I should have believed my brother when he told me not to take that class.
