Mission Statement

This organization is dedicated to encouraging education through the reduction of ineffective educators.

History

SABACT is a newly formed organization dedicated to encouraging education through the reduction of ineffective educators. We were formed on January 26th, 2006 but despite our newness our popularity is quickly increasing. People are quick to realize that this is a worthy cause and they are eager to give us support. If you have any questions about joining the organization or donating time or funds please contact us.
