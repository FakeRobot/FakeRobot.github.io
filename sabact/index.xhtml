Education is a very serious thing. We at SABACT are trying to get to the source of one of the main problems dampering education today. That is, of course:

Boring, Abusive, and Crazy teachers.

If you ask an average student why they do poorly in class their number one answer is that they missed too many classes. We are trying to eliminate that problem by attacking it at the root, the teacher. Students do not skip class if they like their teacher and they do not skip class if they think that their teacher is interesting.

This is why we are trying to eliminate the ineffective eduactors out there. It is in a students nature to want to learn. We must get get rid of the teachers that snuff out that desire to learn.
